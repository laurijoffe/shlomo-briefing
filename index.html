Agent Orchestration Control Plane

Think of modern AI systems like a city's traffic control system. Instead of every car (AI agent) navigating independently, you need a central traffic management center (orchestration layer) that coordinates routes, prevents accidents, and ensures the entire network functions smoothly. The control plane is like air traffic control for AI — it manages which agents get resources, how they communicate, and what happens when things go wrong. Without this coordination layer, you get digital gridlock.
