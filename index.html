ⓘ Teaching Moment

Orchestration vs. Coordination

Think of a restaurant kitchen. Coordination is when the head chef tells each cook what to do—someone works on sauce, someone on protein, someone on plating. Orchestration is when the entire kitchen runs as a system: the pantry auto-refreshes ingredients, the station manager routes orders by priority, and if the grill breaks, the system reroutes work to the fryer. In AI, coordination is agents working together; orchestration is the intelligent system managing their dependencies, state, communication protocols, and failure recovery automatically. The difference mattersWhen you scale from 3 agents to 300.
