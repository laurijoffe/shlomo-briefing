Agent Orchestration

Think of agent orchestration like air traffic control for AI. Instead of one pilot (single AI) trying to handle takeoffs, landings, weather, and cargo all at once, you have specialized controllers: one AI handles scheduling flights, another manages weather routing, and a third coordinates ground operations. The orchestration layer is like the tower that coordinates all these specialists, making sure they share information and work together seamlessly rather than creating chaos in the sky.
