ⓘ Teaching Moment

Hub-and-Spoke Model

Think of Shlomo like a hospital's central ICU rather than a taxi dispatcher. In taxi dispatch (cloud-native orchestration), the hub just routes cars to waiting customers—pure logistics. In ICU, the hub watches every patient monitor, enforces protocols (when IV fluids flow, when surgeons enter), maintains institutional memory (this patient had that reaction before), and catches errors before they cause harm. The orchestration work isn't routing agents; it's embedding decision logic and safety into the coordination layer itself.
