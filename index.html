The Harness Model for Agents

Think of an AI agent like a jazz musician trying to perform. The 'harness' is everything around the musician—the other band members, the sheet music stand, the stage setup, the sound system. Without a good harness, even the best musician (the frontier model) struggles. OpenAI's new Agents SDK adds native sandbox execution (a safe stage where the musician can only touch their assigned instrument), a manifest (agreed-upon sheet music that travels with them), and configurable memory (so they remember what they played yesterday). For Shlomo: this means your agent harness—memory, orchestration, tools, execution environment—needs to be designed alongside the model, not after.
