Teaching Moment
ⓘ Teaching Moment

Memory staleness problem

Imagine a friend who remembers you hate shellfish, but you told them that three years ago. If they bring you shrimp as a gift next week, the memory was accurate but it's now stale—wrong without them knowing it. AI memory systems face this exact challenge at scale: a fact that was true and heavily used becomes wrong without signaling the change, turning confidence into liability. This is why agents need not just retrieval, but verification and decay mechanisms that know when high-confidence facts have expired.
