Hub-and-spoke architecture with isolated spokes

Think of it like a bank with a central teller station (hub) that handles decision-making and shared context, with separate isolated vaults (spokes) for each customer portfolio or business unit. Information flows through the hub for coordination, but sensitive data never leaves its own vault.
