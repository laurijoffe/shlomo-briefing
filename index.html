The Orchestra Model for Multi-Agent AI

Think of a single AI agent like a solo musician—capable, but limited to one instrument's range. A multi-agent system is an orchestra: a conductor (orchestration layer) directs specialized musicians (agents) who play their instrument (domain) in perfect timing. The viola doesn't try to play trumpet parts; it plays what it does best, and the conductor ensures all instruments create harmony instead of noise. In 2026, enterprises discovered that the real value isn't the quality of individual instruments, but the conductor's ability to coordinate them into something greater than any solo could ever achieve.
