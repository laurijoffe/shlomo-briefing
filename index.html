Agent Orchestration

Think of AI agent orchestration like conducting a symphony orchestra. Each musician (AI agent) is skilled at their instrument, but without a conductor coordinating when the violin section plays versus the brass, you get chaos instead of music. Agent orchestration is the conductor layer that manages hundreds of specialized AI agents - telling them when to work, what to prioritize, how to hand off tasks to each other, and ensuring they all follow the same sheet music (your company's policies and data).
