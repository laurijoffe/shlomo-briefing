ⓘ Teaching Moment

The Harness Layer

Think of an AI agent like a Ferrari engine. The engine is powerful (the LLM), but without a steering wheel, transmission, and brakes (the harness), it's just loud metal. The harness is the orchestration layer that decides how context flows, how tools connect, and how decisions get made. Just like a car's harness determines whether you have precision control or catastrophic failure, the agent harness determines whether your AI is reliable, auditable, and trustworthy. Most enterprises have been buying the engine and ignoring the steering wheel.
